Cowboy Game

Click here to view Project
Made over a few months - Sept 2024
This project is a pure passion project for me. I love game development, and this was my first proper attempt at creating a demo. I had much fun learning the ins and outs of the unity engine (which my Godot experience definitely helped with), and also learning how to create complex systems using composition concepts as a key value. This taught me a lot about programming practices, and also a lot about my own personal persistence, since everything is not going to work first try without some immense luck. This gave me plenty of challenges that I absolutely loved figuring out solutions to.

Whilst it isn't a Minimum Viable Product, I think the direciton is clear and measured. The main thing design wise that I struggled with was finding insentives to use the unique ability of the lasso. I eventually figured it out by making it reward you greatly whilst also being slightly difficult to pull off. High risk, high reward.

Who is Callum? :

I'm a new but passionate web developer, based in the UK, West Midlands. I hope to create interesting, clean, functional, responsive and beneficial websites whilst continuously learning more and more about this industry, and about myself. I possess skills in frontend development using HTML, CSS, Javascript and backend development using Python and SQL. I like creating, it is the very core of my life beliefs and I'd be honoured to make this my living, by supporting other/your businesses. Criticism is the key to improvement, and compliments are the key to my heart :)

I am currently taking part in a government funded course, learning skills to support my knowledge in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python and SQL.

A Brief History

My interest in coding started in Year 9, when I was introduced to Python in an IT lesson. I was so interested I went home and willingly researched more about Python (which is unusual for a 13 year old). I battled with deciding what to do for college, but due to mental health reasons (though I wasn't aware of them at the time) I went with Art & Design. It wasn't until I started working that the hobby of coding maintained through my life, and made me realise that this is what I want to do with my life.
